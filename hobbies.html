Things I Enjoy

In addition to my academic studies, I spend a lot of time exploring topics that interest me outside the classroom. Many of my hobbies are closely related to technology, finance, and personal development. I enjoy activities that challenge me intellectually or help me build discipline and consistency.

One of my biggest interests is following financial markets. I enjoy learning about different investment strategies, understanding how macroeconomic trends affect markets, and analyzing companies or sectors. Exploring financial data and evaluating investment ideas helps me better understand how markets function and how investors make decisions.

Another hobby I enjoy is working out and staying physically active. Fitness has become an important part of my routine because it helps improve both physical and mental health. Regular exercise helps me stay focused and maintain energy throughout busy academic weeks. I typically spend several days a week in the gym working on strength training and overall fitness.

I also enjoy learning new technical skills. Whether it is experimenting with new programming languages, building small software projects, or exploring data analysis tools, I like the process of figuring things out and improving my technical abilities. Technology changes rapidly, so continuously learning is an important habit to develop.

Some of My Favorite Activities

  • Analyzing financial markets and investment strategies
  • Strength training and fitness
  • Learning new programming technologies
  • Reading about fintech and digital assets
  • Working on personal tech projects
